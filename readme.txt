Get free events for your location or any other. Filter by categories and deliver interesting content to your visitors. This plugin will deliver entertainment to your visitors and free content for your site!

 

== Description ==

Plugin will get events and insert them as new posts to your WP installation. Every 15 minutes you will get free content and your page will be always up to date.

 

== Installation ==

Just install as any other plugin. Nothing special to consider.
